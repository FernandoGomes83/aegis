{{i18n.labels.derives_from}}: `aegis/framework/security/security-requirements.yaml` — input-validation

- The total request body size SHALL be limited at the middleware level to prevent payload flooding.

### SEC-REQ-HEADERS-01: Security Headers on All Responses

{{i18n.labels.derives_from}}: `aegis/framework/security/security-requirements.yaml` — headers

{{i18n.labels.acceptance_criteria}}:

- A Content-Security-Policy header SHALL be configured with `default-src 'self'` as baseline.
- CORS headers SHALL explicitly enumerate permitted origins and SHALL NOT use wildcard on authenticated endpoints.

{{i18n.labels.derives_from}}: `aegis/framework/security/security-requirements.yaml` — dependencies

{{i18n.labels.acceptance_criteria}}:

- Builds with known critical or high-severity vulnerabilities SHALL fail until remediated.
